Restaurant Preventive Maintenance

Preventive maintenance across locations -- scheduled, tracked, and closed through work orders with photo evidence.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Equipment filter changes (HVAC, refrigeration, hood filters)
  • Gasket inspections and replacements
  • Door hinge adjustments and minor repairs
  • Cleaning of condenser coils and airflow components
  • Basic equipment calibration checks
  • Light plumbing maintenance (faucet aerators, drain cleaning)
  • Light fixture and bulb replacements
  • Tightening loose handles, knobs, and hardware
  • Replacing worn gaskets and seals
  • Fixing minor leaks and drips
  • Adjusting equipment doors and hinges
  • Clearing clogged drains

For major repairs, coordination with specialized vendors is handled through the platform.

Asset tracking

Every piece of equipment is tracked in the GroundOps platform:

  • Equipment make, model, serial number, and install date
  • Complete service history with dates and photos
  • Warranty tracking and expiration alerts
  • Recommended PM schedules based on manufacturer specs
  • Repair cost tracking over time
