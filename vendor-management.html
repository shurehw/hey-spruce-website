Vendor management for multi-location restaurant ops

One intake, one work order queue, documented QA, and monthly scorecards across cleaning, maintenance, and repairs.

Scope

What this service includes

One intake, one queue

Requests become work orders with owner, scope, timestamps, and status -- instead of email threads.

Dispatch and coordination

Access coordination, vendor/crew scheduling, and follow-ups until closure.

Documented outputs

Checklists and photo evidence per visit. Exceptions become corrective actions with owners and due dates.

Monthly scorecards

Rollups by location and vendor: completion, rework, recurring issues, and closure rates.

Standards

Monthly scorecard fields

The reporting layer ops teams use to verify execution and identify recurring issues across locations.

Field What it tells ops
