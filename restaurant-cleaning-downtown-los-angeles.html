Restaurant Cleaning Services -- Downtown Los Angeles (DTLA)

Facilities execution for Downtown LA restaurants with documented QA, checklists, photo evidence, and reporting.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Nightly kitchen and dining room cleaning
  • Deep kitchen cleaning and degreasing
  • Equipment surface cleaning
  • Floor scrubbing and degreasing
  • Restroom cleaning and restocking
  • Hood and exhaust coordination
  • Pre-inspection cleaning
  • Photo documentation per visit

Service is tailored to DTLA-specific conditions: high-rise access protocols, after-hours building management, and varied concept types from food halls to fine dining.

Standards

Health department compliance and documentation

The Los Angeles County Health Department frequently inspects Downtown Los Angeles restaurants. All cleaning is executed to code-driven standards with documented QA per visit.

  • EPA-approved, food-safe cleaning products
  • HACCP-trained teams
  • Photo evidence tied to dates and locations
  • Documentation available for inspections

Priority work orders are available. Response targets depend on trade, time window, and coverage and are defined in the SLA.
