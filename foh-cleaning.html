Dining Room & Front-of-House Cleaning

FOH standards applied consistently across locations, documented per visit with checklists and photo evidence.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Table and chair cleaning and sanitization
  • Booth and banquette cleaning
  • Host stand and waiting area maintenance
  • Entryway and vestibule cleaning
  • Patio and outdoor seating areas (as scoped)
  • Storefront glass -- interior and exterior
  • Interior glass dividers, mirrors, and decorative glass
  • Bar top surfaces and surrounding areas
  • Back bar and bottle displays
  • Bar stools and seating
  • Glassware storage and shelving (as scoped)
  • Floor mats and drain areas

FOH cleaning is matched to service style: fine dining, fast casual, high-volume, etc.

Standards

Floor cleaning and maintenance

Hard floors

Sweeping, mopping, and periodic stripping/waxing for tile, wood, or concrete.

Carpeted areas

Vacuuming and periodic deep carpet cleaning for dining areas.

Entry mats

Cleaning and maintenance of entrance mats and runners.

Programs

Nightly or weekly FOH programs

Nightly after service

Daily FOH cleaning as part of the closing routine.

Weekly deep cleaning

More intensive weekly cleaning for lower-traffic concepts.

Combined BOH + FOH

Comprehensive programs covering both kitchen and dining areas.
