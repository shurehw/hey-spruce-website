Full FOH Cleaning Services

Front-of-house cleaning is more than a quick vacuum. We handle:

  • Table and chair cleaning and sanitization
  • Booth and banquette cleaning
  • Host stand and waiting area maintenance
  • Entryway and vestibule cleaning
  • Patio and outdoor seating areas (as scoped)

We match our FOH cleaning to your service style - fine dining, fast casual, high-volume, etc.

Bar Area Cleaning
