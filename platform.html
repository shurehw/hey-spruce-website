Platform

Facilities execution through one system

Work orders. Dispatch. QA checklists. Photo evidence. Reporting. Across cleaning, PM, and repairs.

Before / After

What the platform replaces

Texts, email threads, spreadsheets, and "someone said they handled it." Replaced by an auditable system of record.

Before

Ad hoc requests. Unclear owners. No closure evidence.

After

Work orders with owners, timestamps, status, and documentation.

Ops outcome

Repeat issues surface fast. Tracked as corrective actions.

Modules

Core modules

Work orders

Every request becomes a trackable work order. Scope, owner, audit trail. Nothing gets lost.

Dispatch

Triage and assign the right crew or vendor. Coordinate access and follow-ups.

QA inspections

Checklists and photo evidence per visit. Exceptions become corrective actions automatically.

Reporting

Monthly scorecards by location and vendor. Completion, rework, recurring issues, closure rates.
