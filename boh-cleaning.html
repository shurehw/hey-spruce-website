Back-of-House Cleaning Services

BOH cleaning executed to a standard, documented per shift with checklists and photo evidence.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Cookline surfaces and stations
  • Saute, grill, and fry station cleaning
  • Prep station surfaces and cutting boards (as scoped)
  • Expediting area and pass window
  • Dish pit and warewashing areas
  • Equipment exteriors, splash guards, and shelving
  • Control panels, knobs, dials, and control surfaces
  • Wall surfaces behind cooklines
  • Vertical surfaces near fryers and grills
  • Floor areas around cooking equipment
  • Behind and under equipment (accessible areas)
  • Baseboards, corners, and floor edges
  • High dusting of shelving, pipes, and overhead structures

Standards

Documentation and QA per visit

Every BOH cleaning session is documented with photos.

  • Timestamped and location-tagged photos for key areas
  • Accessible through the GroundOps dashboard
  • Available for inspections and compliance records
  • Multi-site rollups and scorecards
