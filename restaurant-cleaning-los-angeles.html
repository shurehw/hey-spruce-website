Restaurant Cleaning Services in Los Angeles, CA

Facilities execution across LA locations, managed through work orders with checklists, photo evidence, and exception logging.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Nightly kitchen line and station cleaning
  • Equipment surface cleaning and degreasing
  • Floor sweeping, mopping, and degreasing
  • Food prep surface cleaning
  • Dining room table, chair, and booth cleaning
  • Restroom deep cleaning and restocking
  • Trash removal and receptacle cleaning
  • Photo documentation of all completed work
  • BOH wall, ceiling, and hard-to-reach area cleaning
  • Drain cleaning and sanitization
  • Window and glass cleaning (FOH)
  • Bar area deep cleaning
  • Entry and patio cleaning

Standards

Health code compliance and documentation

All cleaning services are executed to reduce inspection risk with documented standards.

  • EPA-approved, food-safe cleaning products
  • HACCP-trained cleaning teams
  • Color-coded microfiber systems to prevent cross-contamination
  • Temperature logs for sanitizer solutions
  • Photo documentation for compliance records
  • Pre-inspection deep cleaning available
