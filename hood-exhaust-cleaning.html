Hood & Exhaust Cleaning (Coordination & Support)

Hood and exhaust compliance coordination -- scheduled, documented, and auditable through the GroundOps platform.

Scope

What this service includes

GroundOps coordinates with proven hood cleaning providers. The following items are orchestrated depending on your setup:

  • Hood surface cleaning and degreasing
  • Duct and plenum cleaning
  • Fan and rooftop component cleaning
  • Filter cleaning or replacement
  • Overspray protection for FOH and BOH areas

Hood cleaning is aligned with other deep cleaning services to minimize operational disruption.

Standards

Fire safety, air quality, and inspection compliance

Grease buildup is a fire risk. Poor exhaust affects air quality. Inspectors check hoods, ducts, and fans during health department visits.

Service reports

Reports stored and accessible through the GroundOps platform.

Photo documentation

Photos tied to dates and locations for complete traceability.

Inspection readiness

Documentation available for inspections and insurance requirements.

Integration

Combined hood and deep cleaning coordination

Hood cleaning produces the best result when aligned with deep kitchen cleaning.

  • Timing coordinated between providers
  • Pre- and post-clean checks
  • Hood care integrated into the overall maintenance calendar
  • Single-point coordination for all kitchen services
