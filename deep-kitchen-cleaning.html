What "Deep Kitchen Cleaning" Actually Means

Deep cleaning is not a slightly longer nightly service. It's a different level of work.

Our deep kitchen cleaning services include:

  • Pulling out eligible equipment
  • Cleaning walls, floors, and connections behind the line
  • Heavy degreasing of surfaces and fixtures
  • Tile and grout cleaning in high-traffic zones
  • High dusting of overhead structures
  • Focused attention on hard-to-reach corners

We scope each deep clean based on your current condition, menu, and inspection history.
