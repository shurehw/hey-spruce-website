Deep Kitchen Cleaning Services in Los Angeles, CA

A different level of work from nightly cleaning -- equipment pull-outs, heavy degreasing, and surface restoration scoped per location.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Pulling out eligible equipment
  • Cleaning walls, floors, and connections behind the line
  • Heavy degreasing of surfaces and fixtures
  • Tile and grout cleaning in high-traffic zones
  • High dusting of overhead structures
  • Focused attention on hard-to-reach corners

Each deep clean is scoped based on current condition, menu type, and inspection history.

Standards

Quarterly and semi-annual cadence

Deep cleaning frequency is determined by volume, menu type, and inspection history.

  • Quarterly deep kitchen cleaning for high-volume concepts
  • Semi-annual deep cleaning for lower-volume locations

Cadence factors: foot traffic, ticket volume, menu type (heavy fry/saute vs. light prep), past inspection notes, and shifts per day.

Inspection prep

Health inspection preparation

When an inspection is scheduled, deep cleaning can be prioritized to address the areas that carry the most risk.

Previously cited areas

Focus on areas flagged in past inspections.

High-risk surfaces

Food contact surfaces and grease buildup zones receive priority attention.

Restroom and FOH

Restroom and front-of-house areas included as needed.
