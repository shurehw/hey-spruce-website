Commercial Floor Scrubbing & Degreasing

Floor scrubbing and degreasing scoped to surface type, documented per visit with photo evidence.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Auto-scrubbers for large open areas
  • Walk-behind scrubbers for medium-sized floors
  • Manual scrubbing for tight spaces or delicate surfaces
  • Specialty equipment for tile, concrete, or epoxy floors
  • Lifting and cleaning under floor mats
  • Degreasing mat surfaces and edges
  • Drain area and surrounding floor cleaning
  • Grout line and tile edge scrubbing near drains

Equipment is matched to flooring material for effective cleaning without damage.

Standards

Chemical treatment and safety protocols

Heavy degreasing

Industrial degreasers for kitchen floors with heavy grease buildup.

pH-balanced cleaners

Safe for food service environments and gentle on flooring materials.

Floor-specific products

Matched to tile, concrete, epoxy, or sealed surfaces.

Slip resistance

  • Grease removal reduces slip hazards during service
  • Complete chemical removal so floors are not slippery after cleaning
  • Optional slip-resistant coatings or treatments for high-traffic areas (as scoped)

Programs

One-time or recurring floor programs

Weekly floor scrubbing

High-volume kitchens benefit from weekly machine scrubbing.

Bi-weekly or monthly

Standard cadence for moderate-traffic locations.

Deep clean / one-time

Post-construction, pre-inspection, or one-off resets.
