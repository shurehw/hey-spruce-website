Machine Scrubbing & Manual Cleaning

Depending on your floor type and condition, we use:

  • Auto-scrubbers for large open areas
  • Walk-behind scrubbers for medium-sized floors
  • Manual scrubbing for tight spaces or delicate surfaces
  • Specialty equipment for tile, concrete, or epoxy floors

We match equipment to your flooring material so we clean effectively without damage.

Chemical Treatment & Degreasing

Los Angeles restaurant floors accumulate grease fast. We use:

Heavy Degreasing

Industrial degreasers for kitchen floors with heavy grease buildup

pH-Balanced Cleaners

Safe for food service environments and gentle on flooring materials

Floor-Specific Products

Matched to tile, concrete, epoxy, or sealed surfaces

Mat Cleaning & Drain Areas

Mats and drains need regular attention:

  • Lift and clean under floor mats
  • Degrease mat surfaces and edges
  • Clean drain areas and surrounding floors
  • Scrub grout lines and tile edges near drains

These are the areas inspectors look at closely.

Slip Resistance & Safety

Clean floors are safer floors. Our process includes:

Grease Removal

Proper Rinsing

Slip-Resistant Coatings

Optional coatings or treatments for high-traffic areas (as scoped)
