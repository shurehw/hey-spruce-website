Equipment-Specific Cleaning Procedures

Deep Fryer Boil-Out Procedure

  1. Turn off fryer and let cool to 150¬∞F
  2. Drain oil completely (dispose properly)
  3. Remove and clean baskets separately
  4. Fill with water to normal oil level
  5. Add fryer cleaner per instructions
  6. Bring to slow boil for 20 minutes
  7. Turn off and let cool
  8. Drain and rinse thoroughly
  9. Wipe dry with clean towels
  10. Refill with fresh oil

Flat-Top Grill Cleaning

  1. Turn off and let cool to 150¬∞F
  2. Scrape surface with grill scraper
  3. Apply grill cleaner or oil
  4. Scrub with grill brick in circular motion
  5. Wipe clean with damp cloth
  6. Apply thin layer of oil to season
  7. Clean grease traps underneath
  8. Wipe down exterior surfaces

Ice Machine Monthly Cleaning

  1. Turn off machine and empty ice
  2. Remove and wash parts per manual
  3. Mix approved ice machine cleaner
  4. Run cleaning cycle
  5. Rinse thoroughly
  6. Sanitize with approved sanitizer
  7. Run 2-3 batches and discard
  8. Return to normal operation

Problem Likely Cause Solution

Los Angeles Specific Requirements

üå¥ LA County Special Considerations

  • Outdoor Dining Areas: Must be cleaned daily, pressure washed weekly
  • Earthquake Preparedness: Secure all chemical storage, anchor equipment
  • Water Conservation: Use low-flow pre-rinse sprayers, fix leaks immediately
  • Grease Disposal: Must use licensed hauler, keep manifests 2 years
  • Air Quality: Clean hood filters weekly during high pollution days
  • Pest Control: Monthly service required (not quarterly)
  • Food Handler Cards: All staff must have valid cards
  • Allergen Awareness: Separate cleaning tools for allergen-free areas
