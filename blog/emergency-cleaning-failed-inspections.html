Specific Solutions for Common Violations

1. Temperature Violations (Most Common)

Problem: Cooler Above 41¬∞F

  • Immediate: Move food to working cooler
  • Check: Door seals, thermostat setting, coils
  • Fix: Clean coils, adjust thermostat, repair seals
  • Prevent: Daily temp logs, monthly coil cleaning

Problem: Hot Holding Below 135¬∞F

  • Immediate: Reheat to 165¬∞F or discard
  • Check: Equipment calibration, proper use
  • Fix: Repair/replace equipment, train staff
  • Prevent: Hourly temperature checks

2. Pest Evidence

Problem: Cockroach Activity

  • Immediate: Call pest control for emergency treatment
  • Deep Clean: All cracks, behind equipment, drains
  • Eliminate: Water sources, food debris, cardboard
  • Seal: All cracks and entry points
  • Document: Get pest control report for inspector

Problem: Rodent Evidence

  • Immediate: Remove all droppings with proper PPE
  • Sanitize: All affected areas with strong solution
  • Seal: Holes with steel wool and caulk
  • Set: Traps and bait stations (by professional)
  • Remove: Food sources and nesting materials

3. Sanitation Failures

Problem: Dirty Food Contact Surfaces

  • Stop: All food prep immediately
  • Wash: All surfaces with hot soapy water
  • Rinse: With clean water completely
  • Sanitize: Proper concentration, correct contact time
  • Air Dry: Don't towel dry sanitized surfaces

Problem: Grease Buildup

  • Degrease: All surfaces with commercial degreaser
  • Scrub: Walls, equipment, floors
  • Hood: Clean or schedule professional cleaning
  • Maintain: Implement daily degreasing routine
