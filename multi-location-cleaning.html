Multi-Location Restaurant Cleaning

Cleaning and facilities execution through one queue and one reporting layer, with work orders documented per visit.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Nightly kitchen and dining room cleaning
  • Quarterly or monthly deep cleaning
  • Hood and exhaust coordination
  • Floor scrubbing and degreasing
  • Restroom maintenance
  • Pre-inspection cleaning
  • Emergency cleaning services

One partner for all facilities needs instead of a patchwork of local vendors.

Standards

Onboarding, platform setup, and consistent execution

Onboarding

All locations assessed, custom scopes built, teams trained on brand standards.

Platform setup

All locations added to the dashboard with schedules, checklists, and reporting.

Consistent execution

Same processes at every location, photo-documented every time.

Integration

Preventive maintenance and vendor management included

  • Preventive maintenance: equipment PM, filter changes, light repairs
  • Vendor coordination: hood cleaners, pest control, HVAC, etc.
  • Emergency dispatch: SLA-based escalation for urgent repairs
  • Asset tracking: equipment logs, warranty tracking, service history

Scalability

Scaling with growing brands

  • Add new locations without RFPs or vendor searches
  • Same cleaning standards and processes from day one
  • Platform access for regional managers and corporate teams
  • Custom reporting for audits and compliance

Concepts served

Fast casual chains

Quick-service and fast-casual concepts with high volume and rapid expansion.

Full-service restaurants

Casual dining and upscale concepts with complex BOH and FOH needs.

Ghost kitchens and virtual brands

Delivery-focused concepts operating from shared or dedicated facilities.
