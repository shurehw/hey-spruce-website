Nightly Restaurant Cleaning Services

Each visit is a work order with a checklist, photo evidence, and exceptions logged as corrective actions.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Kitchen line and station cleaning
  • Equipment surface cleaning and degreasing
  • Floor mopping and spot cleaning
  • Dining room table, chair, and booth cleaning
  • Restroom cleaning and restocking
  • Trash removal and receptacle cleaning

Scope is customized to concept, volume, and menu.

Standards

Consistency and accountability

Assigned teams

The same crew is assigned to your location so they learn the space and workflow.

Photo documentation

Every session is documented with timestamped photos confirming completed work.

Checklist tracking

Standard checklists ensure nothing is missed across consecutive shifts.

Programs

BOH, FOH, or full-restaurant programs

BOH only

Kitchen and back-of-house areas.

FOH only

Dining room, restrooms, and guest areas.

Full restaurant

Complete nightly cleaning of all areas. Preferred by multi-location chains for vendor consolidation.

Scheduling

Flexible timing around your operations

After closing

Cleaning begins after the last guest leaves.

Between shifts

For 24-hour or late-night concepts, cleaning during slow periods.

Early morning

Before prep teams arrive for breakfast or lunch service.

Multi-location

Nightly programs across multiple sites

  • Consistent cleaning across all sites
  • Same checklists and standards everywhere
  • Centralized billing and reporting through one platform
  • Scale up or down as locations open or close
