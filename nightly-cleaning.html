Nightly Restaurant Cleaning Services - Los Angeles

Nightly cleaning managed like operations, not "a crew." Each visit is a work order with a checklist, photo evidence, and exceptions logged as corrective actions.

What "Nightly Cleaning" Includes

Nightly cleaning is your consistent baseline. We handle:

  • Kitchen line and station cleaning
  • Equipment surface cleaning and degreasing
  • Floor mopping and spot cleaning
  • Dining room table, chair, and booth cleaning
  • Restroom cleaning and restocking
  • Trash removal and receptacle cleaning

The scope is customized to your concept, volume, and menu.

Consistency & Accountability

The problem with nightly cleaning is consistency. We solve it with:

Same Team

We assign the same crew to your location so they learn your space

Photo Documentation

Every session is documented with photos so you know the work was done

Checklist Tracking

Standard checklists ensure nothing is missed night after night

BOH + FOH Cleaning Programs

You can choose:

  • BOH Only: Kitchen and back-of-house areas
  • FOH Only: Dining room, restrooms, and guest areas
  • Full Restaurant: Complete nightly cleaning of all areas

Most multi-location chains prefer full-restaurant programs for simplicity and vendor consolidation.
