Restaurant Restroom Cleaning Services

Restroom cleaning that holds up to inspection and brand standards, documented per visit with checklists and photo evidence.

Scope

What this service includes

  • Toilets and urinals thoroughly cleaned and sanitized
  • Sinks, faucets, and countertops
  • Mirrors and glass surfaces
  • Partition walls and doors
  • Soap and paper towel dispensers
  • Trash receptacle cleaning and liner replacement
  • Complete floor cleaning with EPA-approved disinfectants
  • Tile and grout cleaning to remove stains and buildup
  • Detail cleaning of baseboards and corners

Standards

Restocking and supply management

Restocking is included as part of cleaning service. Supplies are provided by the client.

  • Paper towels and toilet paper
  • Hand soap and sanitizer
  • Seat covers (if applicable)
  • Air fresheners

Guest and staff restrooms

Guest restrooms

High-touch, high-traffic areas requiring frequent attention.

Staff restrooms

Maintained to the same standard as guest-facing facilities.

ADA and family restrooms

Specialized attention for larger or accessible facilities.

Programs

Frequency based on traffic and volume

Nightly cleaning

High-volume restaurants with heavy restroom traffic.

Weekly deep cleaning

Moderate traffic locations with existing daily maintenance.

Bi-weekly or monthly

Supplemental deep cleaning alongside an existing program.
